Pig Dice

An exiciting two player dice game. Each round, player roll dice to determine score. If it's a one, reset the score of the round to zero, player turn end. As long as the roll is not one, the player can choose to continue or end round. If player choose to end the round, the score for the round is added to their total. Players alernate turns until they hit the score goal that they set.
